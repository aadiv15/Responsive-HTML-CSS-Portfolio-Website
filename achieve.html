Resume

Disclaimer: This is just going to be a page where I boast of all my achievements, small or big. If you find it annoying that's on you ;)

During my school years, I was consistently an academically bright student with a healthy amount of interest and participation in co-cirricular activities. Aside from my inclination towards Literature, Science, Maths and Data Sciences, being involved in other activities played a pivotal role in shaping my personality and confidence. These included painting, public speaking, debating, writing, music, and in a few instances, dancing; my achievemnts in all these spheres can be seen below. Additionally, I was elected as the head-girl of my school for the academic session 2016-17. As the head-girl I had manegerial and coordination duties which I completed satisfactorily with the extremely valuable support from teachers and an amazing student-council team.

  • DANCING

While dancing isn't something I was actively involved in, when an opportunity came, I took it. Catchcing up with my friends was tough in the beginning, but I took it as a challenge and soon was able to follow up well. My efforts paid off as our team bagged the 3rd prize in the city-level folk dance competition in the senior category. Aside from that, I took part in group performances in some of the events our school conducted. Overall, I take all of this as an indication of my ability to try new things out and do well in them.

I'm still learning but this just goes on to show that if I decide to do something, I will do it regardless of the circumstances. Click here to watch a few of my covers.
