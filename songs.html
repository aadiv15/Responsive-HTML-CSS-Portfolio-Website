Resume

Songwriting is something I was always interested in. While I used to scribble a few lines here and there in my notebooks when I barely knew anything, this interest took a rigid shape only in 10th standard when I wrote my first complete song. I wrote two more soon after my board examinations and finally decided one day to show them to my parents and tell them this is something I want to do. Today, this is what I do in most of my free time. However, due to privacy/copyright issues, I can't upload my songs here yet. Apologies!
